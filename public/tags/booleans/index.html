While I primarily have been focusing on my current employment and freelance projects, I also have made incremental changes to existing packages/repos on my GitHub. In particular, I have merged my VBAOps and LOBOps repos, as the latter is a translation of the former. As such, I have a new repo called BASops, which serves as the primary repo where I will write and maintain utility functions for dialects of BASIC, such as VBA and LibreOffice Basic (LO Basic).
